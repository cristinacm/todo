Argumentc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/todo/tododfa#Argument

Participant that is involved in the event described by the Frame. It can be Core (its presence is compulsory to convey the event) or Optional (its presence is not compulsory, but it adds additional information).
Is defined by
https://w3id.org/todo/tododfa
Term status
stable
Example
Frame: Losing

ArgumentSet:
* Owner [Core]
* Possession [Core]
* Place [Optional]
* Time [Optional]
...

For the sentence "My mother lost her watch at home":
"[My mother - owner(C)] LOST [her watch - possession(C)] (at home - place (O))"
has super-classes
is disjoint with
Action c, ArgumentSet c, FomaGrammar c, Frame c, FrameHead c, Intent c, Skill c

actionTagdp back to ToC or Data Property ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/todo/tododfa#actionTag

String that identifies the Action when invoked in a SystemRequest/Response (DomainONT).
Is defined by
https://w3id.org/todo/tododfa
Term status
stable
Example
System request: "Do you need me to {argumentTag}?"

Action: take_guideRobot
actionTag: "take you somewhere"

Resulting SysRequest: "Do you need me to {take you somewhere}?"
System request: "Necesitas que {argumentTag}?"

Action: take_guideRobot
actionTag: "te lleve a algún lugar"

Resulting SysRequest: "Necesitas que {te lleve}?"

argumentConnectordp back to ToC or Data Property ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/todo/tododfa#argumentConnector

String that defines the word that acts as a connector between the Argument and the rest of elements in a SystemRequest/Response.
Is defined by
https://w3id.org/todo/tododfa
Term status
stable
Example
SystemRequest: Do you want me {actionTag}{argumentConnector}{WorldElement}?

Action: take_guideRobot
argumentConnector: "to"

"Do you want me {to take you}{to}{the restroom}?"
SystemRequest: ¿Necesitas que {actionTag}{argumentConnector}{WorldElement}?

Action: take_guideRobot
argumentConnector: "a"

"¿Necesitas que {te lleve}{a}{la sala}?"
