currentValuedp back to ToC or Data Property ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/todo/tododt#currentValue

When asking the user for a specific information, the information currently asked to the user.
Is defined by
https://w3id.org/todo/tododt
Term status
stable
Example
UserReq: "I want to go to the room"

possibleValue "room1"
possibleValue "room2"

SystemReq: "Do you want to go to {room1}?" (currentValue: room1); "Do you want to go to {room2}? (currentValue: room2)"
UserReq: "Quiero ir a la sala"

possibleValue "sala1"
possibleValue "sala2"

System Req: "多Quieres ir a la {sala1}?" (currentValue: sala1); "多Quieres ir a la {sala2}? (currentValue: sala2)"
